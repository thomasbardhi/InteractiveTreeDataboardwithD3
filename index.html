Tree Analysis in Lawerence, Ma

By Thomas Bardhi

Welcome to the Lawrence Tree Dashboard!

This dynamic dashboard is crafted with data from the urban forest of Lawrence. Here's what you can explore:

  • Geospatial Map: A detailed map pinpointing every tree in Lawrence. Overlay it with a heatmap for a vivid depiction of tree density.You can also use the select feature to filter by year
  • Interactive Charts: Start by brushing across the dashboard. This interaction will reveal:
    • A Bar Chart illustrating the frequency of various tree diameters.
    • A Nested Pie Chart differentiating between:
      • Medium to Large Trees: Red slice for trees with a diameter of 3 feet or more. The outer pie shows species counts.
      • Small Trees: Blue slice for trees with a smaller diameter. You’ll see species and their counts for this category too.

Interact with the dashboard and uncover the story of Lawrence’s trees. See how different species contribute to the canopy and discover the distribution of tree sizes.
